Donate Blood And Save Lives

Every year thousands of patients die due to unavailability of blood. Almost every medical surgery procedure requires the presence of blood pints to compensate for the loss of blood in the patient during the medical procedure. Due to the lack of donors, less amount of blood is available making it hard for patients to get blood of the specific blood group, not only this, the lack in supply of blood also leads to increase in prices of blood pints.
